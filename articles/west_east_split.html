The East-West Split

Around 390 before Christ, the city of Rome was invaded by a Gallic tribe.

After this, the city would not be invaded by a foreign force for another 800 years - a remarkable feat in ancient history, or perhaps any history.

At the time of the invasion, Rome was still not the empire that we think of today. It was really just a regional power within Italy.
